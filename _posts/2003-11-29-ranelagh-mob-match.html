--- layout: post title: Ranelagh Mob Match ---

Ninety nine runners turned out for the Ranelagh mob match on Saturday. We had a good start - packing nine in the first ten. However the blues packed the middle order well so the final outcome was in doubt right until Jenny Thomas closed the team. Well done to all who turned out - particularly to Ashley Brutnall, Andy Lea-Gerrard and Emily Linklater who were making their debut for Thames. Full results below.
