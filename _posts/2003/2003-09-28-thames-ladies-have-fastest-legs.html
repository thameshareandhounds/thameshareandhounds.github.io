--- layout: post title: "Thames Ladies have fastest legs" ---

Sonia O'Sullivan and Benita Johnson ran the fastest legs of the day at the Ladies Southern relays.

Sonia recorded the fastest official time of the day to propel the Thames A team to 10th spot. She was backed up by Kate Symons, Sarah Bentley and Rachael Thompson.

New signing Benita Johnson ran even faster, though eligibility requirements meant she competed for the B team. Her official time of 11:43 would have clipped one second off the previous course record, held by none other than Paula Radcliffe.
