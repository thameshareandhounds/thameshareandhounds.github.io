--- layout: post title: "Surrey Veterans XC Championship" ---

The V60 team continued their winning ways with victory in the Surrey Veterans Champs in Richmond Park on Saturday.

Led home by Keith Spacie and Robin Dixon who won their respective M65 and M60 titles, the team closed ahead of Herne Hill and Bels. The club also had success in the V50 team, taking bronze behind SLH and Herne Hill. Mike Farmery finished third in the bronze.
