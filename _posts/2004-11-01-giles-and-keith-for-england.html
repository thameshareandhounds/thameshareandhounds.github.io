--- layout: post title: Giles and Keith for England ---

Congratulations to Peter Giles and Keith Spacie on their selection for England in the British and Irish Veterans Cross Country International later this month at Croydon. Peter and Keith, by the captains estimation, are the sixth and seventh first claim Thames member to receive international honours this year after Allie Outram, John McFarlane, Matt Lynas and, of course, Benita and Sonia. Second claim, Lucy Hasell has also been selected to run for England at Margate later this month.
