--- layout: post title: Surrey League - Men Division 1 Results ---

Alistair Moses from Reigate led the 158-strong field home, making light work of the hills and the infamous Butts at the end of each lap. In the team, the home side took full advantage of local knowledge as Herne Hill closed in second ahead of a slightly weakened Belgrave. At the foot of the table, Ranelagh closed the gap on Aldershot, leaving the field wide open for the January and February races. Results below, and thanks to Wendy Petty and all of the course pioneers and helpers for organising a splendid day.
