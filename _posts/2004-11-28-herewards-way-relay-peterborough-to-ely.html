--- layout: post title: Herewards Way Relay, Peterborough to Ely ---

A team of Thames veterans came 25th out of over 100 teams over 40 miles of Herewards Way on Sunday. Anna Garnier brought us in 43rd on the first leg to Whittlesey, after which Michael Johnson gained one place on the longest leg to March. Rhodri (whippet) Davies gained a fine 18 places on the 3rd leg to Welney, leaving it to Peter Blair-Fish to see the team into Ely.
