--- layout: post title: 2004 Varsity Cross Country Races ---

Fraser Thompson completed a hat trick of victories while Alice Beverly led from the front as Oxford completed a double victory in the 114th Varsity Cross Country race hosted by Thames on the Common on Saturday. Reversing the Cambridge double victory of last year, the men are now all square on 57 victories each, while Oxford ladies extend their lead to 5 points - 17 points to 12. Nick Talbot and Julia Bleasdale ran out winners in the old boys and girls race. Individual results from all the races below.
