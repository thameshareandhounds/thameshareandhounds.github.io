--- layout: post title: "HHH Mob Match victory" ---

In a (hoped for) rehearsal for next week, Thames ran out victors over Herne Hill on a windblown and wet Common. Simon Wurr had a dazzling run to lead the field home, helped a little by Matt Norminton running a longer interpretation of the final mile. Congratulations also to Chris Chataway and Jeffrey Gordon who both celebrated their birthdays with fine times. Results below.
