--- layout: post title: "Great North Run" ---

Benita Johnson won the ladies race in impressive style, setting a new Personal Best of 67.55, while Sonia OSullivan finished strongly to take 6th place in 68.55. In a day of good results, Chris Chataway ran 1.39 for provisional 1st V70 while Dave Barker (75.04) and Rob Bowyer (85) also ran well.
