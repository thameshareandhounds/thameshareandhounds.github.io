--- layout: post title: "Light Blue Wash" ---

The rival attraction of the Irvine wedding meant that the Thames turnout was lower than previous years. This, coupled with a fine Cambrdige team meant that the saltires were brushed aside by the light blues who dominated the event in the team performances. Individually things were brighter, with particularly strong runs by two of our old Cambridge members, Corin Hughes and Julia Bleasdale who finished a fine 16th in the long race. Matt Jones and Julia Fea from dark blue stock also ran well. Full results below.
