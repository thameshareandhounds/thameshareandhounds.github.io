--- layout: post title: Garnier grinds it out in the Grunty Fen ---

Anna Garnier won the V50 race in the annual Grunty Fen half marathon in 95:10, finishing 18th out of 150 women in the race. Flat, windy and boring was the description of the event so get your entries in for 2006...
