Oxford won both individual races and both team events in a highly dominant display. In the Ladies event captain Courtney Birch front ran to a fine victory whilst in the Mens event Jonathan Blackledge surged clear inside the final 200 metres after an epic race. Strong packing by both Ladies and Mens teams rounded off a dominant day for Oxford.
