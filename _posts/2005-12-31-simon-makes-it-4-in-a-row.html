--- layout: post title: Simon makes it 4 in a row ---

The Captains attempt to dominate the world of multi-terrain running took another giant leap forward with a victory in the Huncote Hash in Leicestershire by nearly 3 minutes. Anna Garnier built on her fine run of form with 4th place in the Ladies field (61st overall). Ashley Brutnall also donned his studs and cruised round in 101st. 208 finished.
