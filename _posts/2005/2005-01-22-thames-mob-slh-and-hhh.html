--- layout: post title: "Thames mob SLH and HHH" ---

With nearly double the number of Thames runners from the same fixture two years ago, the 50 strong Thames pack ensured a comprehensive victory over South London and Herne Hill Harriers. Ian Harkness and Nicola Don led us home, with other good runs from Michael Goatley and Dan Pescod. Congratulations also to Evan Bond and Andy Johnson who were making their Thames debut and to Al Irvine who made his V40 debut.
