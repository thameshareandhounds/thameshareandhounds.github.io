--- layout: post title: "Cambridge give Thames the blues" ---

The Cambridge Ladies and Mens teams posted strong performances exactly a month before the Varsity Match. Rachel Disley yet again excelled herself with a fine victory in the Ladies race whilst David Symons led the Thames mens team home. Nic Gould set a PB for the course and newcomer Sean Donohue ran well on his Thames debut in 26th. Despite our best attempts to engineer a scoring system to allow Thames to appear competitive no suitable methods could be found.
