--- layout: post title: 10 mile challenge ---

A hardy bunch of Thamesmen survived the icy wind of the 10 mile challenge. Nick Altmann led home Nick Malynn and Larry Mathews, all of them having added some supplementary mileage with a variety of off-piste excursions from the unmarked course. Matt Bryant cleverly stuck to master pioneer Michael Worthington to secure the handicap prize.
