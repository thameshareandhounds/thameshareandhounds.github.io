--- layout: post title: Ranelagh Mob Match ---

Thames won both the Ranelagh mob match and the 18-to-score event between all five clubs present held to celebrate 125 years of running from host club Ranelagh. At the sharp end Thames provided a 1-2-3 with John Macfarlane Ian Harkness and Nick Altmann showing a clean pair of heels to the field whilst Naomi Warner made a welcome return to cross country to place first lady ahead of some strong opposition. Mob matches however can only be won with a big team performance and everyone who ran played their part in two fine wins for the club.
