Ben Reynolds set a new PB and finished first Brit / third M40 in a fantastic 2h 26.35. Passing 20 miles in 1h 50.03 Ben was on track for his 2:24 target but he lost a small amount of time in the run in to the finish. Sonia O Sullivan was 14th in the ladies race in 2h 42.05
