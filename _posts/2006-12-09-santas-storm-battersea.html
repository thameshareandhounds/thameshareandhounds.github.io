--- layout: post title: Santas Storm Battersea ---

Tom Kingsnorth showed he is more at home in a Santa suit than in waist deep ponds by taking victory in the Battersea Park Santa Run ahead of David Symons. James Whetman also ran well for 5th spot with Victoria Richmond completing the Thames quartet.

1.	Tom Kingsnorth	19.52
2.	David Symons	20.12
5.	James Whetman	22.02
553	Victoria Richmond	37.25
