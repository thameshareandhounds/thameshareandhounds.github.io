--- layout: post title: Southern Vets Cross Country Championships ---

Henk Altmann won individual gold in the M65 category and led Peter Giles and Michael Johnson to a well earned team gold in the M60 event. Mike Farmery ran well in the M50 event, leading Rhodri home. Hugh Shields and Chris Cheeseman flew the saltire and put in strong runs in the M40/45 race. Rachel Haining and Vicky Filsell were well up in the Ladies race; full details to follow.
