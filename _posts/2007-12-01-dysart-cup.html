--- layout: post title: Dysart Cup ---

An impressive contingent of Thames ladies did battle in the Dysart Cup event in Richmond Park. We were just pipped to first place in the team event by Serpentine, but there were fine runs from Liz Stavreski (6th), Alison Carpenter (10th), the Captain (11th), Vikki Filsell (15th), Enfys Jones (19th), Rachel Haining (30th) and Loran Celiku (40th).
