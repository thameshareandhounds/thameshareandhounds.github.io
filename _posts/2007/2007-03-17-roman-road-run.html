--- layout: post title: "Roman Road Run" ---

A solid group of Thames members competed over the 9.5 mile Roman Road Run hosted by Cambridge University. Julia Bleasdale and Sophie Wood showed they were recovering well from injury and the rigours of travelling respectively, taking the first two spots in the ladies race against a strong group of students. Darren Talbot was first Thames home to take the Cheshire Tally-Ho trophy, while Richard Clarke retained the James Ryan trophy as the first Thames vet.
