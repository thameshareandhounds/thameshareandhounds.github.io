--- layout: post title: "Wimbledon Common Time Trial" ---

Dave Symons continued his recent good form with a fine victory in 16:56, followed again by Ian Higgins and Richard Pitt in 3rd and 4th respectively. Also present were the Ladies Captain (13th – 2nd lady), Alan Gibbons (15th), Anna Garnier (18th – 21:22 – course PB), Lorna Celiku (35th – 23:47 – course PB), and Colin Price (43rd).
