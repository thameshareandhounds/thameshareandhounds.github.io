--- layout: post title: Man Versus Horse, Wales ---

In the words of the Sunday Times "One of the toughest horse races in the world was yesterday nearly won by a man in running shoes and shorts. After powering over 22 miles, three mountains and two rivers, John McFarlane came in a close second in the Man Versus Horse race at Llanwrtyd Wells in Wales. Though McFarlane finished 30 seconds behind the winner, a horse called Dukes Touch of Fun, he beat 45 other horses and more than 300 runners". John then had an early start on Sunday morning to win his King of the Mountains leg in the Welsh Castles Relay....
