--- layout: post title: Run to the Beat / Great North Half Marathon ---

John Macfarlane splashed his way to victory in foul weather in a time of 1:10.12 and Lucy Macalister placed second lady (and thirteenth overall) just behind local rival Birhan Dagne in 1:19:19. Meanwhile, up in sunny Newcastle, Benita Johnson recorded 1:14:31 for eleventh spot in the elite ladies race.
