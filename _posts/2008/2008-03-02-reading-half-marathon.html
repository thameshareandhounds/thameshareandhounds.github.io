--- layout: post title: "Reading Half Marathon" ---

There were some swift runners in action this weekend over traditional pre London race the Reading Half, and the Thames men ran well with Phil running 1:06:12 for 7th, John MacFarlane 1:08:08 for 12th and both Toby Pearson and Chris Wrighton running sub 1:13:00 for 44th and 45th places.
