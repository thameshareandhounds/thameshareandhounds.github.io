Some highlights from recent events at the WCTT. On 7 June Ian Higgins placed 2nd in 18:15, with V50 Anna Garnier 1st lady in 21:12. On 14th June new member George Garnier ran an impressive 16:48 to place 3rd, a couple of seconds slower than his 3rd place performance 3 weeks beforehand. Finally, on 21 June Anna finished 1st lady once more this time in a pb of 20:59, the race also witnessing a course PB from John Bryant in 27:56.
