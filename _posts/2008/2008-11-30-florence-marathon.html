The TH&H ladies put in some awesome running this weekend at the Florence Marathon with pbs all round. Liz Stavreski beat the 3 hour barrier that she has been working so hard to acheive, placing eighth lady in 2:56:45, followed by Vikki Filsell with a pb of 3:22.
