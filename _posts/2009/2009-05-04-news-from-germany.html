--- layout: post title: "News from Germany..." ---

News reaches us that Alex is running well in Germany, winning both the short and long distance courses at the Middle Saxony cross country championships with just a two hour rest between the two races. Moving onto the road Alex won the Imp Celle 10km road race and the North German 10km Championships both in sub 37min, before making her half marathon debut with a fine 1:23:12 at Berlin.
