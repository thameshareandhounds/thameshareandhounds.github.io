--- layout: post title: Ranelagh Mob Match ---

A fantastic turnout of 51 Thames runners turned out against Ranelagh and tooks us to victory against our hosts. Martin Shore led the field home with a great performance and there was a wave of white behind him with Thames taking the first 15 places. Ranelagh packed well in the top 30 but after that we matched them throughout the field, winning by 600 points in the end. Results can be found on Ranelagh's website here
