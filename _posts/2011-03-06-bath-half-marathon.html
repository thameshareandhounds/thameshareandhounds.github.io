--- layout: post title: Bath Half Marathon ---

The Thames ladies continued their winning ways, the TH&H ladies team of Antonia Burt, Ruth Clifton and Claire Day all ran pbs and placed second ladies team (behind Kenya). Lucy Macallister, running for first claim B&W,placed fourth lady, also with a pb, finishing close on the heels of sole TH&H male representative Gordon Pearce.

Gordon Pearce		1.13.41

Antonia Burt		1.22.10
