--- layout: post title: OUCCC Iffley Road Mile Meeting - Wednesday 3rd August ---

OUCCC invites all Thames members to its Iffley Road Mile Meeting on Wednesday 3rd August.

The Iffley Road Mile Race

Your chance to run a high quality mile race at the historic home of the 4 minute mile
Wednesday 3rd August- first race at 7:30 p.m.
The evening will be a series of mile races graded according to 1500m pb. Top races (at least) to have pace makers.

Entry £3 in advance or £5 on the day.
