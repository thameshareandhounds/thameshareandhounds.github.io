--- layout: post title: World Trail Championships ---

Julian Rendall was selected for the Great Britain & Northern Ireland team for the World Trail Championships, held in Connemara, Ireland, on 9th July

The race was 70km in distance and consisted of a combination of trail, bog, grassland and some very tough mountains.

Many congratulations to Julian, who finished 16th overall (in 7 hrs 16 mins), and second GB&NI runner. GB&NI finished fourth.
