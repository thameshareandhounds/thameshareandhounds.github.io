--- layout: post title: Wedding Day 7km Race, Bushy Park (29 July) ---

Thames took the men's and women's team events at the Wedding Day 7km Race in Bushy Park on 29 July.

Many congratulations to James McMullan, who led the men's team home in third, and to Ruth Clifton, for a fine run to win the ladies' race in 27:09.
