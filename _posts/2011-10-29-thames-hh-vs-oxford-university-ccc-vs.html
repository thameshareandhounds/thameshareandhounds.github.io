--- layout: post title: Thames H&H vs Oxford University CCC vs Stragglers ---

Oxford ran out easy winners in the three-way match with Thames and Stragglers. Luke Caldwell was quickest on the day, taking advantage of dry conditions to finish in a fine time of 38:23. At the front for Thames, it was a case of captains future, present and past, with Matt Jones getting tantalisingly close to the 40-minute barrier.
Matt's performance won him the Long Distance Challenge for 2011. The Ladies' Long Distance Challenge was won by Ladies' Captain, Anna Garnier.
In the ladies' race, Hayley Munn for Oxford was first home in another fine time of 22:36.
