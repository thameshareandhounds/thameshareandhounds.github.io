--- layout: post title: 38th Thames H&H vs Ranelagh Harriers Mob Match ---

Thames scored a good victory in the traditional Mob Match against our friends and rivals from Richmond Park. Martin Shore took his second successive victory in the race in 39:36 - taking advantage of unseasonally dry conditions to register a very quick time.
With 43 Saltires in action and strong packing throughout the order, it was one of our biggest victories in the recent history of the fixture. Many thanks to all who ran.
