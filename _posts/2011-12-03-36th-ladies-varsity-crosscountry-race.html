Oxford registered their fifith win in a row by 14 points to 25. Lucy Gossage of Cambridge was first home, running strongly to finish in 23:17. However, with five of the six Oxford runners lined up behind her, the team result was never in doubt for the Dark Blues.
They now lead the series by 23 wins to 13.
