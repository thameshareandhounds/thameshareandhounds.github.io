--- layout: post title: The Grim Challenge ---

Thames scored a 1-2-3 in the Grim Challenge on Sunday, an 8.5 mile multi-terrain race held on an MOD tank-testing site, with mud, water hazards and camouflage nets to negotiate. Simon Wurr set a new course record to lead the home field with new member, Richard Kowenicki, a fantastic second, and Tom Samuel, making a welcome return to racing, in third. Ashley Brutnall continued his rapid return to form also breaking the hour. 1,945 runners finished.

1 Simon Wurr TH&H 50.23
2 Richard Kowenicki TH&H 50.46
3 Tom Samuel TH&H 50.50
31 Ashley Brutnall TH&H 58.11
