--- layout: post title: South of the Thames Championship Race ---

Thames hosted the South of the Thames Championship Race over a two-lap 7.5-mile course on the Common. Star of the day was Ruth Clifton, who followed up her inter-counties performance, with another excellent run to take the ladies' title.
