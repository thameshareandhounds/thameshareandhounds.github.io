--- layout: post title: "Fleet Half Marathon" ---

It was half marathons galore for Thames runners today with those at Reading joined by four more at Fleet. Martin Shore led the way in 71 minutes for fourth, closely followed by a pb from Matt Jones and a sub 75 run from Chris Wrighton. Slightly behind was Nick Altmann having completed a duathlon the day before.
