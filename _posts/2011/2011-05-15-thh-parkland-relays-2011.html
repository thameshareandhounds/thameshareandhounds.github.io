--- layout: post title: "TH&H Parkland Relays 2011" ---

The TH&H Parkland Relays will be taking place on Wednesday 1st June in Richmond Park, starting at 7.00 p.m.

The format is 4 x 3 mile legs for men and 3 x 3 mile legs for ladies.

For further details, please contact Simon Molden at simon@scmolden.co.uk.
