--- layout: post title: "World Masters' Stadia Championships" ---

Ben Reynolds secured a bronze medal in the V45 5,000m in the World Masters' Championships in Sacramento, California

Ben reports that he lost out to a Spaniard and Russian in a last lap sprint, finishing in 15:27.94.
He was reasonably pleased with the results given the usual extenuating circumstances (hot, windy, jet lag, getting too old, no speed left, etc).
