Five Saltires were in action in the Elmbridge 10km race in 24 July. Congratulation to our new vice-captain, Matt Jones, who finished in an impressive third place. It was a family affair as brother, Stevhen Jones, took the Quentin Clough handicap, ahead of Matt and Lilly Griffiths.
