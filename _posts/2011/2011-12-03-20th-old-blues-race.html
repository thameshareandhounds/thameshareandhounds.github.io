Oxford scored a convincing win in the 20th Old Blues' Race, by 48 points to 181 in a 9-to-score match. Full results are below and, as is custom, only ex-students scored in the team event.
David Bruce - the 2009 Blues' Race winner - was first home and Courtney Bishop - the 2005 and 2007 winner of the Ladies' Race - was the first woman home.
Oxford now leads the series by 12 wins to 8.
