--- layout: post title: "Southern Masters' Cross-Country Championships, Lloyd Park (men)" ---

The M40 team took the gold medal in the Southern Masters' Cross-Country Championships at Lloyd Park on Saturday. Andy Morgan-Lee led the way with a superb run for second place, only three seconds off first place. He was ably supported by Andy Robinson and Ben Reynolds to take the team event by four points from Newham & Essex Beagles, for whom second-claimer, Stuart Major, ran well for fifth.
The M50 team took eighth place overall, with Jerry Watson finishing fifth in the individual event. It was a big running day for Jerry who then hopped over the Common to take part in the Old Boys' Race.
