--- layout: post title: ECCA Cross-Country Championships (men), Parliament Hill ---

The Thames men's team finished 18 out of 125 teams at a sunny Parliament Hill on Saturday. In the 9-to-score race, they finished even higher in 8th. There were excellent runs from Robin Brown, Mike Causer and Richard Koewnicki to lead the team home.
