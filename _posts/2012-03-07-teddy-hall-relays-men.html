--- layout: post title: Teddy Hall Relays (men) ---

Thames finished 11th out of 98 teams at the Teddy Hall Relays. The course was longer than usual (4.4 miles), going the long way around Christ Church Meadow. There were good runs from all, including two legs from the captain and Robin Brown bringing the team home having already completed a leg for CUH&H.
An OUCCC Old Gits' team, including Andy Arbour and Ranelagh's Peter Haarer finished 28th.
