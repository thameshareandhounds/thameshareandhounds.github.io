--- layout: post title: 32nd London Marathon ---

A excellent array of Saltires were in action in marathon on Sunday. Andy Weir led the way home in 2:31:51 and he way closely followed by the vice-captain, Matthew Jones, and Julian Rendall.
On the ladies' side, Ruth Clifton once again ran very well to finish in 2:53:47.
Further down the order, the President once again completed the course, accompanied by his son, Matthew; and Jeffrey Gordon kept up his ever-present record.
Chris Finill - running in the colours of Harrow AC - kept up his record of finishing every London Marathon in under three hours.
