--- layout: post title: British Athletics League National Division 2, Basingstoke ---

Three Saltires were in action in the colours of Kingston & Polytechnic. In the 5000m, Gordon Pearce finished 4th in the A string in 15:53 and Brian Thursby-Pelham finished 2nd in the B string in 16:20. Andy Arbour finished 6th in the 3000m steeplechase in 11:24.
