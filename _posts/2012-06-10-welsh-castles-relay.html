--- layout: post title: Welsh Castles Relay ---

The team of 20 Saltires has just returned from another very enjoyable Welsh Castles weekend. The team finished 16th overall. Particular highlights were Gordon Pearce’s victory on stage 1 and Ben Reynolds’ 2nd place on the first mountain stage (stage 3). Many thanks to all who ran, ferried and supported across the weekend.
