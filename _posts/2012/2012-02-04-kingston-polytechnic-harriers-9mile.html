--- layout: post title: "Kingston & Polytechnic Harriers' 9-mile Race, Richmond Park" ---

Thames ran out winners in a three-sided race against Kingston & Polytechnic Harriers and Milocarians at an icy Richmond Park.
Mike Causer pulled away from Andy Weir in the final run-in to take the individual honours, with Andy Arbour - running in the colours of K&P - finishing fourth, after a battle with his team-mate, Mark Reed.
