--- layout: post title: "Teddy Hall Relays (ladies)" ---

Jo Davies and Anna Garnier, plus Gail Jones (from Headington) who kindly guested, finished 13th (out of 33) teams at a windy Teddy Hall Relays. The course was picturesque, if a bit wet, and the runners were supported by lots of cheering Magdalen College School boys.

Leg 1 Joanna Davies 29.03
Leg 2 Anna Garnier 29.14
Leg 3 Gail Jones (Headington - guest) 36.09
