--- layout: post title: "Percy Stenning Memorial Run" ---

Nick Altmann, Claire Day, Nic Gould, Annette Chase, David Symons and Will Cockerell (joining later) - set on their way by the captain - traced the 20-odd miles from Putney Heath to Godstone to commemorate the 120th anniversary of the death of Percy Stenning, one of Thames' early members and winner of the first four National Cross-Country Championships (1877-80).
