--- layout: post title: "Surrey Masters Cross-Country Championships W45, Richmond Park" ---

The Thames W45 took the bronze medal in the Surrey Masters Cross-Country Championships in Richmond Park. Pippa Major led the way home in 5th, following by Anna Garnier in 8th and Vikki Filsell in 9th.
Anna's performance meant she also retained the individual W55 title for another season.

1 Sue McDonald SLH 24:44
2 Susanna Harrison Woking 25;17
3 Kay Trinder Woking 25:25
5 Pippa Major THH 25:46
8 Anna Garnier THH 26:32
9 Vikki Filsell THH 26:54

Match score:
South London Harriers - 20 points
Stragglers - 21 points
Thames H&H - 22 points
