--- layout: post title: "40th Thames H&H vs Ranelagh Harriers Mob Match" ---

Thames ran out convincing winners in the 40th Mob Match with Ranelagh Harriers over our friends' course in Richmond Park. We were dominant at the front of the race with 25 of the first 30 home and then we scored solidly throughout the rest of the order to win by 1,356 points to 2,566, the second largest margin in the series (after 2004).
Will Mackay capped an excellent debut run in a Saltire to take the individual honours in 42 minutes, 7 seconds. Ruth Clifton was first lady home in 47 minutes, 43 seconds and 21st overall. Another very strong run from Ruth.
Many thanks to all who turned out to make this a great day for Thames.
