--- layout: post title: "LADIES 3rd in SURREY LEAGUE but retain lead by 40 points" ---

Short of a few runners, the THH Ladies did their stuff on a lovely open course in Nonsuch Park, Cheam on Saturday. Just 12 points covered the top three teams.

Ruth Clifton again attracted just 2 penalty points to finish 2 seconds behind Tish Jones of Belgrave. Nicole Clifford (V40) surprised herself having said it was very hard, finishing behind Ruth in 3rd place. Next league match, what with Belgrave and W4H snapping at our heels we are determined to get everyone out and hopefully expand our lead. WELL RUN TO ALL THE LADIES WHO TOOK PART.
