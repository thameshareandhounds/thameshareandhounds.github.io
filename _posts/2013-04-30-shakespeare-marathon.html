Simon Wurr makes his debut at the marathon distance

The Shakespeare marathon took place in Stratford-upon-Avon and Simon Wurr tried the distance on the road for the first time. Simon put in a negative split by just over 2 minutes and won the race in 2hr 40:51. Having seen Simon yesterday he looked as fresh as a daisy so I have the feeling there is a lot more to come at this new distance.
