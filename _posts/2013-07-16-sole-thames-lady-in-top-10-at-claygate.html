--- layout: post title: Sole Thames Lady in top 10 at Claygate 5 on Sunday 14th July ---

Joanna Shillington runs well to take 8th spot in this annual event.

Well done to Joanna for flying the saltire. We normally have quite a few ladies running in this fun event in Surrey but with the outgoing captain injured and Maureen Poole too, neither of them were able to keep Joanna company.
