--- layout: post title: "'WE DID IT' THAMES LADIES TAKE THEIR 4th SURREY LEAGUE VICTORY" ---

Their team spirit is second to none; their pursuit of fun; their desire to win; their sheer determination and true grit.... These qualities were all demonstrated on Saturday at the final fixture of the Surrey League. Victory was not certain with two very strong teams snapping at the THH Ladies' heels. However without some of our speedy runners, the only way to win was to graft very very hard. Everyone deserves a mention from the first fixture to the final one. Ruth yet again, did us proud with a superb run over a very hilly course at Denbies and leading the field home. Her second victory in a row. We will be opening the champagne very soon.
