--- layout: post title: "Southern Championships, Parliament Hill" ---

Due to the postponement of the race from 26th January, meaning unavailability of a number of runners, plus a string of injuries, it was a very select Thames contingent that travelled to Parliament Hill. Chris Daniels ran well to finish just outside the top 100, with Anthony Doolittle well inside the top 200.

1 Frank Tickner Wells City 48.10
2 Neilsen Hall Bedford & County 49.14
3 Darren Deed Bedford & County 49.37
102 Chris Daniels TH&H 58.25
175 Anthony Doolittle TH&H 61.58
