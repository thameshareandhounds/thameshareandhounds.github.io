It was very much Cambridge's day in the Varsity Match, with comfortable victories for the Lights Blues in both the ladies' and gentlemen's races. Cambridge's win in the 38th ladies' contest was their largest since 1984 and their win in the 123rd gentlemen's race was their largest since 1995.
There was a small compensation for Oxford in that they carried off the Old Blues' Race for the eighth year in the row.
