--- layout: post title: Thames come 4th in the English Half Marathon Championships in Reading ---

Thames had a stonking team out for the English Half Marathon Championships at Reading on Sunday, with an unconfirmed 4th team placing. Simon Wurr and Richard Kowenicki put in some great preparation for London by finishing 42nd and 43rd respectively in 70:07 and 70:08. While Ben Reynolds was 71st in 72:32; Brian Thursby Pelham 94th in 74:04 and Anthony Doolittle 284th in 81:45.
