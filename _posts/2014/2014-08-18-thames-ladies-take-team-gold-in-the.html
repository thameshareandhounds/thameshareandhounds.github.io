--- layout: post title: "Thames Ladies Take Team Gold in the Surrey Road 5km Championships" ---

Many congratulations to Claire Day, Amy Aronson and Dorchie Cockerell who won the Surrey Road 5km County Championships yesterday. On the hilly course the girls packed well with Claire running 18:42 in 5th; Amy 18:51 in 6th and Dorchie 19:07 in 9th.
