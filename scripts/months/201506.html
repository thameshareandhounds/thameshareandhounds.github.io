Last Friday 5km Race, Hyde Park - 26/6/15

In sweltering conditions, Simon Molden finished second in the June Last Friday 5km Race in Hyde Park in a time of 16.49.

Robin Dickson's funeral details are as follows: - 18/6/15


Robin Dickson's funeral will take place on Tuesday 7th July. There will be two services. A brief service at Reading Crematorium at 2.30pm (55, All Hallows road, Caversham, RG4 5LP.)

Then at 3.30pm there will be a longer service of thanksgiving for Robin's life at the Caversham Baptist Church, South Street, RG4 8HY.

Family flowers only. Donations to Guide Dogs for the Blind for those that wish, either on the day, or send to the funeral directors AB Walker & son, Caversham.

After the service, there will be tea laid on in the reception room of the church.
